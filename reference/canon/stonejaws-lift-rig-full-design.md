## Core Identity

**Stonejaw's Lift Rig** is Dragulies's famous loader-type Geodeum mech: a modified heavy labor frame with Craton steampunk industrial styling, inherited from the previous Crown holder, Stonejaw.

It is not a sleek military machine. It is a construction-born arena relic, a public inheritance, and a machine with work still trapped in its bones.

```text
Stonejaw's Lift Rig is the burden with arms.
It was built to lift stone. The Geodeum taught it to lift verdicts.
```

## Tyranosapien Operator Fit

The cockpit is an **operator cradle**, not a human seat.

It must account for:

```text
large saurian torso
forward-leaning stance
tail accommodation or rear tail channel
wide hip and thigh clearance
non-human shoulder mechanics
shorter but powerful Tyranosapien arms
clawed hands
large clawed feet
neck and snout clearance
restraint systems that do not assume human anatomy
```

## Arena Modifications

Preserve labor-machine ancestry. Arena changes may include reinforced loader arms, arena plating, Crown Stonebound sigils, stone-scored shoulder guards, ring plates from earlier matches, pressure-release vent flares, impact bells, protective operator cage, clamp guards, stabilizer cleats, and old work serial marks.

If it threatens, it threatens by weight, pressure, pinning, lifting, blocking, shoving, forcing space, and controlling the ring.

Avoid default guns, missiles, sleek military parts, or smooth futuristic mecha styling.
